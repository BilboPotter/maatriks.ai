How to delete your {{appName}} account

You can delete your {{appName}} account at any time from within the mobile app. This page explains the in-app path, what happens after deletion, and how to contact support if you cannot access the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the {{appName}} app on your device
  2. Go to Settings (accessible from your profile)
  3. Scroll to Account
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

What happens when you delete your account

When you confirm account deletion:

  • Your account credentials and profile information are permanently removed
  • Your workout logs and training history are deleted
  • Your AI-generated workout feedback and related planning data are deleted

Data retention

After deletion, your personal data is removed from our active systems. Deleted data may remain in encrypted backups for up to 30 days before permanent removal. We may retain de-identified aggregate information that does not identify you.
