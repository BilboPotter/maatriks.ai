Password reset requests should be started from the mobile app sign-in screen. The reset email will then send you back through the public handoff page and into the app.

How to start the reset

  1. Open the {{appName}} app
  2. Go to the sign-in screen
  3. Tap Forgot password
  4. Enter the email address tied to your account
  5. Open the reset email and continue from the recovery link
